# ffxi-content
* various content for an old dead MMO
  * one of those adjectives also describes me, but thankfully just one for now

## contents
* several things you will not find elsewhere, but you don't need
  * a couple things that are not my original work (buzzoff etc.) but do contain modifications to work better and/or more broadly
  * many things are broken (sandworm!) or not even remotely complete (monstrosityinstincts and wallfacer were both abandoned far under 5% completion)
* a couple things that are at least somewhat functional:
  * trailblazer: for when you really want to spend bayld on 20 pickaxes!
  * bsm: do you desire to hear me say LEARNED when you learn a blue magic spell?
  * pricelogger: ever wondered how much you're getting from NPC'd garbage?
  * pronountrouble: the goal here was to integrate this into dramagen.lua because that had a hardcoded "he", but I never got around to it
  * discerningeye: while not fully updated, makes A Discerning Eye way less painful - there's a bit more automation I haven't put into this yet to save you having to run commands, but you can handle two lousy console commands.
  * chocorace: just automated button presses and big sleeps. someday the person who has the packet-based one will finally release it and obsolete this overnight
  * pirates: want to know when there's pirates? and/or have your character automatically run to the deck rail and start up fisher to catch a fish. you know, one or the other or both. scope creep just kicked in.
  * coalitions: originally written by an unknown author, modified by someone smarter than myself, here you go, it's an actual thing that is sketchy but it's old tech
